Overview

Hoffen Trade International is a business entity located at Dhaka, the capital city of Bangladesh. It started its operation before 5 years. By this time Hoffen became the Business Partner of Carl Zeiss (Medical and Microscopy Division) and Brainlab of Germany, Staar Surgical of USA, iCare and Centervue (Italy) of Finland, Biotech Vision Care of Switzerland, Karl Kaps of Germany, The Magstim of UK and Technomed Europe, Moptim of China, YiSUMEDI of S. Korea, Karl Kaps of Germany, Sidapharm of Greece, MiCo BioMed Co. Ltd. (TJ30, LLC) of S. Korea, Acon Biotech (Hangzhou) Co. Ltd. (Augmenta Global Ltd.) of China, Surescreen Diagonostics Ltd. of UK and Tiss'You Biologics of Itali for Bangladesh market. The company is considered as one of the leading suppliers of medical equipment, devices and IOLs in Bangladesh market with appropriate product registration from the Government. Hoffen is also a partner with BMTF of Bangladesh Military for supply of machineries, spares and consumables. As a First-Class Contractor, Hoffen's one of the sister concerns 'Gateway International' is engaged in river dredging and inland water transportation and industrial Zone related engineering construction works with BIWTA and BSCIC of the government respectively. It is also engaged in supply of environmental equipment for various environmental projects of Bangladesh Government and the World Bank.

To be leading in the medical equipment, devices & Intraocular Lens (IOLs) market in Bangladesh by providing reliable and quality product with prompt and professional after sales service through relationship with a view to ensuring success of our valued clients and Business Partners.
